# Video Placeholders for Forsythe Landing Page

## Required Video Files
Place the following video files in this directory:

### Main Demo Video
- hero-demo.mp4 (2-3 minutes) - Full demo video showing AI callback process

### Service-Specific Ads (10 seconds each)
- roofing.mp4 - Roofing contractor ad
- hvac.mp4 - HVAC services ad  
- plumbing.mp4 - Plumbing services ad
- solar-10s.mp4 - Solar installation ad
- landscaping.mp4 - Landscaping services ad
- coffeeshop.mp4 - Coffee shop/retail ad

## Video Specifications
- Codec: H.264
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (landscape) or 1080x1920 (vertical)
- Bitrate: ~5000 kbps
- Format: MP4
- Audio: AAC, 192 kbps
- Duration: 5-10 seconds for ads, 2-3 minutes for demo

## Thumbnail Images
Create corresponding .jpg thumbnail images in the thumbs/ subdirectory:
- roofing.jpg
- hvac.jpg
- plumbing.jpg
- solar.jpg
- landscaping.jpg
- coffeeshop.jpg

The landing page is configured to reference these videos. The page will work without them (showing placeholders), but for full functionality, add the actual video files.
