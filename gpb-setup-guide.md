## Best Practices for Your Google Business Profile

### 1. Keep Information Consistent
- Ensure your NAP (Name, Address, Phone) matches exactly across all directories
- Use "Forsythe Publishing & Marketing" as your exact business name

### 2. Encourage Customer Reviews
- Ask satisfied clients to leave reviews
- Respond to all reviews professionally
- Aim for 4.5+ star rating

### 3. Update Regularly
- Post updates 1-2 times per week
- Keep business hours accurate
- Update services when needed

### 4. Monitor Insights
- Track how customers find your profile
- Monitor which actions they take
- Adjust strategy based on performance data

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Inconsistent NAP Information:** Make sure your name, address, and phone number match exactly on your Google Business Profile as on your website and other directories.

2. **Incomplete Profile:** Fill out all sections completely to maximize visibility.

3. **Not Adding Photos:** Visual content greatly improves click-through rates.

4. **Ignoring Reviews:** Both positive and negative reviews need to be acknowledged professionally.

5. **Wrong Categories:** Use the most specific categories that apply to your business.

## Tracking Success Metrics

Monitor these key metrics in your Google Business Profile dashboard:
- Number of views
- Number of clicks for directions
- Number of phone calls
- Number of website visits
- Review rating and volume
