## Review Request Timing Strategy

### Initial Request: Day 7
- Send immediately after first week of AI voice agent operation
- Include initial performance metrics
- Focus on installation and setup experience

### Performance Review: Day 14
- Share early performance improvements
- Emphasize specific metrics that have improved
- Request review of results achieved

### Success Confirmation: Day 30
- Highlight 30-day performance results
- Show ROI calculations if available
- Focus on business impact

### Milestone Reviews: Day 60, 90
- Highlight long-term performance
- Share case study opportunities
- Emphasize sustained results
