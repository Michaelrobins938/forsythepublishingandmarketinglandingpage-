## Implementation Strategy

### Pre-Event
1. Contact event organizers 2-3 months prior
2. Propose value-add beyond standard sponsorship
3. Prepare branded presentation materials
4. Schedule live demo capabilities
5. Set up event-specific landing pages

### During Event
1. Engage with attendees about AI voice technology
2. Capture leads with event-specific codes
3. Schedule live AI demo callbacks
4. Network with potential partners
5. Document for social media and case studies

### Post-Event
1. Immediate follow-up with AI voice call
2. Send customized thank-you gifts
3. Share photos and outcomes on social media
4. Provide metrics to event organizers
5. Develop relationships with new contacts

## Measuring Success

- Return on sponsorship investment
