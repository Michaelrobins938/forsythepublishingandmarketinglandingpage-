## Response Best Practices

### Timing Guidelines:
- Respond within 24 hours of review publication
- Acknowledge review within first sentence
- Personalize with customer's business type and location
- Mention specific metrics from their review when possible
- Include contact information for private resolution when needed
- Keep tone professional but genuine
- Reference DFW market challenges/commonalities

### Key Elements to Include:
- Local market reference (DFW, Dallas, Fort Worth, specific city)
- Industry reference ([service type] business)
- Appreciation for time taken to review
- Reference to specific benefit/result mentioned
- Professional commitment to excellence
- Invitation for direct communication if needed

### Phrases to Use Frequently:
- "Competitors miss these opportunities"
- "Fast-paced DFW market"
- "Dallas-Fort Worth service business community"
- "Local [service type] businesses"
- "Lead conversion excellence"
- "Instant response technology"
- "24/7 coverage for Texas weather"
- "Meeting North Texas market demands"

### Avoid:
- Generic responses
- Defensiveness
- Too much technical detail
- Promises not yet fulfilled
- Blaming customer for issues
- Public complaints about internal processes

These templates will help ensure consistent, professional, and locally-focused responses that enhance your reputation among DFW service businesses while showing Google that you actively engage with customer feedback.
